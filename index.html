Superconducting Quantum Interference Device (SQUID)

Lenny, the film's main character, hustles his product SQUID. He insists, "This is not like, ‘TV, only better.’" Instead, he emphasizes that "[t]his is life. This is a piece of somebody’s life. Pure and uncut, straight from the cerebral cortex. I mean, you’re there, you’re doing it, you’re hearing it, you’re feeling it."

Lenny's SQUID headset allows people to literally experience the feelings of others. Applications in the movie include comitting a crime or pleasure. The film uses phrases like "wired" and "jacked in," which makes the technology seem like a drug. It's a particularly addicting escape because the movie's reality is lawless and chaotic.

Nerve Online Reality Game

In this universe, Nerve is an online game where "watchers" pay "players" high sums of money to complete the dares. The "players" must obey the following rules.

  1. All dares must be documented and recorded on the player's phone

  2. If players fail or don't complete the dare, they lose the money earned

  3. Players cannot report the game to the police

Although the game starts out with low-stakes dares, the plot thickens when the main characters are forced to take on bigger and bigger dares.

Big Brother

In George Orwell's prediction of 1984, society is controlled by the ruling party Ingsoc. Ingsoc retains their power through an authoritarian surveillance state and is represented by a mascot called Big Brother.

In the novel and film adaptation, Ingsoc retains power over its people by using telescreens to closely monitor everyone.

The phrase "Big Brother is watching you" is used to keep citizens in line.

Zola's Algorithim & Project Insight

Former evil scientist Arnim Zola developed Zola's Algorithim while working for SHIELD (who people thought were the good guys). The algorithim takes in public and private data from individuals to predict whether they pose a threat to the world.

In this universe, the World Security Council developed Project Insight to eliminate the individuals who the algorithim deemed dangerous in the name of security.
